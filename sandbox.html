Trendy Hipster Date
Description:

I fell in love with this date format when i first opened up the Day One iPhone app. I like that it feels old school, but it's just unique to me. You don't normally see it. It might not be the best way to display a date format but i love it. I've decided I want to use it in my blog & my lessons. I was going to use it on J&L but that fell through thanks to WordPress.

Example:
Mon10
October 2011
What I Did:

Well, i pretty much just copied Jake's Code, but, the gist is this: I created a div and gave it the class of "date" inside of date i made 2 more divs, in the divs are spans. One div is the rotated day & number, the other is the month & year. Each span has a class on it & each class is getting some styles. The major styles happen in div #1 where the dat is rotated & the number font-size is increased to 40px; It was really pretty simple.
